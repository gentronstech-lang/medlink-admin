Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026
App: Medlink Africa (com.medlinkafrica.app)

Medlink Africa (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the Medlink Africa mobile application and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app as a patient, doctor, or ambulance/emergency driver.

1. Information we collect

Depending on your role and how you use the app, we may collect:

  • Account information: full name, email address, phone number, password (stored securely hashed), profile photo, gender, date of birth, and role (patient, doctor, or driver).
  • Authentication data: one-time passwords (OTP) for phone verification; Google or Apple sign-in identifiers when you choose social login.
  • Health-related information: patient profile details (e.g. weight, height, blood group, emergency contacts), consultation notes, prescriptions, lab reports, and messages exchanged with healthcare providers.
  • Doctor professional information: specialization, experience, clinic name and address, medical license documents, availability, and consultation fees.
  • Driver/ambulance information: vehicle details, license information, mission and trip data.
  • Location data: precise location when you use emergency SOS, ambulance tracking, or location-based services (with your permission).
  • Payment information: payment transaction details for consultations and ambulance services; payout/bank card details for doctors and drivers who add a payout account (we store only what is needed to process payments — full card numbers are not stored in plain text).
  • Communications: in-app chat messages, consultation-related content, and push notification tokens (FCM).
  • Device and usage data: device type, operating system, app version, IP address, and general usage logs for security and service improvement.

2. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you;
  • Enable appointment booking, teleconsultations (chat and video), and e-prescriptions;
  • Provide emergency SOS and ambulance dispatch and tracking;
  • Process payments for healthcare and transport services;
  • Send appointment reminders, emergency alerts, and service notifications;
  • Verify doctor credentials and manage platform safety;
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to support requests;
  • Improve, secure, and maintain our services.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only as needed:

  • Between users: e.g. patients and assigned doctors, or patients and ambulance drivers during an active emergency or trip.
  • Service providers: cloud hosting, payment processing (e.g. Stripe), SMS/OTP providers, push notifications (Firebase), video/voice (e.g. Agora), maps, and analytics — only to perform services on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements: when required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with appropriate safeguards.

4. Data retention

We retain your information while your account is active and as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize personal data except where retention is required for legal, tax, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution purposes (e.g. payment records may be kept for up to seven years where required by law).

To delete your account, see our Account Deletion page.

5. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encrypted data transfer (HTTPS/TLS) in production, access controls, and secure password hashing. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we encourage you to use a strong password and keep your login credentials private.

6. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or update your profile information in the app;
  • Delete your account and associated data (via in-app deletion or by contacting us);
  • Withdraw consent for optional features (e.g. location, notifications) through device or app settings;
  • Contact us with privacy questions or complaints.

Email: support@medlink-africa.com

7. Children’s privacy

Medlink Africa is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

8. International users

Medlink Africa serves users in Africa and other regions. Your data may be processed in countries where we or our service providers operate. We take steps to protect your information in line with this policy.

9. Third-party links and services

The app may link to third-party services (e.g. Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, payment providers). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. We encourage you to review them.

10. Medical disclaimer

Medlink Africa helps you connect with healthcare professionals and emergency services. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. In a life-threatening emergency, contact your local emergency services immediately.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version at this URL and update the effective date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
