Account & Data Deletion

App: Medlink Africa
Contact: support@medlink-africa.com

How to delete your account

Option 1 — In the app (recommended)

  1. Open Medlink Africa and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile / Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account (or “Permanently remove account”).
  4. Confirm and enter the OTP sent to your registered phone or email.
  5. After verification, your account is deleted.

Available for Patient, Doctor, and Driver/Ambulance accounts.

Option 2 — By email

If you cannot access the app, email support@medlink-africa.com with:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Your registered email or phone number
  • Your role (Patient / Doctor / Driver)

We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days.

Data that is deleted

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Profile photo and uploaded documents (e.g. medical license)
  • Patient, doctor, or driver profile information
  • Appointment and consultation data linked to your account
  • Chat messages associated with your account
  • Payout/payment method details stored in your profile
  • Push notification tokens and linked Google/Apple sign-in

Data that may be retained

DataReasonRetention
Payment/transaction records Legal, tax, fraud prevention Up to 7 years (or as required by law)
Anonymized analytics Service improvement Indefinitely (no personal identification)
Support emails about deletion Proof of request Up to 2 years

After deletion, you cannot sign in with the same account. A new account requires new registration.
